Our Story


Ice Cream is Our Passion


Happy Day Dessert Factory is a new and exciting brand and 12+ years of experience in the frozen dessert industry has been used to place a fresh twist on a familiar treat. Founder Galen Moore Jr. has been working with ice cream his whole life.



Our Services


Happy Day Dessert Factory serves Premium Homemade Hard Ice Cream as its signature product in addition to Water Ice, Frozen Soft Custard, Stackers (frozen custard layered with water ice), Ice Shakes (blended mix of water ice and custard), Twisters (blended mix of water ice, premium hard ice cream or custard, and cookie pieces), milk shakes, sundaes, fresh baked cookies, ice cream sandwiches, ice cream cakes, frozen pies, waffle cones and bottled water.



Products Made Daily


Our products are made daily, in house with premium ingredients. We also offer healthier options like our fat and cholesterol free water ice for customers.
